Decription

Sambal ikan bilis is one of the side dish for Malaysia's national dish, Nasi Lemak. However, people also eat Sambal Ikan Bilis with plain rice and with Roti Canai. Sambal ikan bilis tastes spicy due to the chillis and has that saltiness coming from the anchovies. Some people like to make Sambal Ikan Bilis very spicy, and some prefer the spiciness to be moderate and just nice. It depends on people's spicy tolerance.
